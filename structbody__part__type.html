◆ hit_difficulty

float body_part_type::hit_difficulty = 0.0f

How hard is it to hit a given body part, assuming "owner" is hit.

Higher number means good hits will veer towards this part, lower means this part is unlikely to be hit by inaccurate attacks. Formula is chance *= pow(hit_roll, hit_difficulty)
