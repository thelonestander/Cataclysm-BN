20/**
21 * @name BODYTEMP
22 * Body temperature.
23 * Body temperature is measured on a scale of 0u to 10000u, where 10u = 0.02C and 5000u is 37C
24 * Outdoor temperature uses similar numbers, but on a different scale: 2200u = 22C, where 10u = 0.1C.
25 * Most values can be changed with no impact on calculations.
26 * Maximum heat cannot pass 15000u, otherwise the player will vomit to death.
27 */
