25// event_tansformations and event_statistics are both functions of events.
26// They are intended to be calculated via a stats_tracker object.
27// They can be defined in json, and are useful therein for the creation of
28// scores and achievements.
29// An event_transformation yields an event_multiset, while an event_statistic
30// yields a single cata_variant value (usually an int).
31// The values can be accessed in two ways:
32// - By direct calculation, by calling stats_tracker::get_events or
33// stats_tracker::value_of.
34// - On a 'live updating' basis, by calling stats_tracker::add_watcher.
35//
